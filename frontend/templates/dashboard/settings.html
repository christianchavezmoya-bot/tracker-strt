Map Coordinate Setup

Link your floor plan to real-world GPS using two known corners (e.g. survey lat/long labels printed on the drawing).

  1. Click Start, then place each corner on the map (click again to adjust).
  2. Press Enter coordinates when the marker is in the right spot.
  3. Repeat for the second corner, then save.

Southern hemisphere latitudes are negative (e.g. -32.214525). Eastern longitudes are positive (e.g. 149.808612).
